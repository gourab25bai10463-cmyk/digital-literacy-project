### **Project Overview: Digital Literacy \& Cybersecurity Awareness**

This project serves as a comprehensive guide for university students—specifically those in a B.Tech (CSE AI \& ML) track—to navigate the modern digital landscape with technical proficiency and a "Security-First" mindset. Given the high-speed nature of campus life at VIT Bhopal, the project bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world digital survival.



#### 1\. **The Scenario**

The project is framed through the lens of a 19-year-old student residing in a hostel. This environment presents unique digital challenges:



* **Constant Connectivity**: Relying on shared hostel Wi-Fi for academic projects and competitive coding.



* **Financial Velocity:** High frequency of UPI-based transactions for everything from mess fees to canteen snacks.



* **Professional Launchpad:** The immediate need to build a credible portfolio on platforms like Kaggle, GitHub, and CodeChef to secure AI/ML internships.





#### 2\. **Core Pillars of the Project**

The report is structured into four functional modules:



* **Programming Foundations:** Demonstrating algorithmic logic using Python to solve competitive coding problems and pattern printing (e.g., Right-Angled Triangles).



* **Professional Communication:** Mastering the "Soft Skills" of the digital age, including formal email etiquette for faculty and industry coordinators.



* **Data Engagement:** Using Google Forms to conduct primary research, such as the "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz," highlighting the importance of data integrity in AI.



* **Cyber Defense:** Analyzing real-world threats like UPI "Collect Request" fraud and establishing a "Zero-Trust" protocol for personal and financial safety.





#### 3\. **Key Objectives**

* **Identity Building:** Crafting a professional digital footprint that stands out to recruiters.



* **Risk Mitigation:** Identifying social engineering red flags before they lead to financial or academic loss.



* **Tool Mastery:** Proficiency in industry-standard platforms (CodeChef, Kaggle, Google Workspace).



* **Reporting Literacy:** Understanding the legal and official channels for reporting cybercrime in India (Helpline 1930 and cybercrime.gov.in).



##### **"In a world driven by AI, the most critical code we manage is our own digital reputation and security.**
